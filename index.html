About Me

Hi, I'm Mark, a full stack developer based in Austin, TX.

From a young age I have had a deep curiosity and desire to understand the world. I love to think. I love to analyze things. It was natural, then, that I would end up studying physics in college.

While in school, I began tutoring high school students and discovered my passion for teaching, which I followed into special education.

I have spent the bulk of my professional career at Texas School for the Blind & VI, working in the residential department. There I worked with a wide variety of students, focusing mostly on living skills. I was drawn the intellectual challenge of breaking down and communicating ideas, but mostly I loved the human connections I made there.

In the back of my mind, however, was the desire to do something more analytical. I have always been excited by technology and the possibilities it creates. I decided to start learning coding and I was quickly hooked. I enrolled at a Coding Bootcamp at UT Austin and was greatly challenged and greatly fulfilled. I can't wait to start my new professional life!

My Portfolio

Battle Math

Battle Math is an educational role-playing game designed for elementary students to hone their foundational math skills. It is built with a React front end and a Node Express/MySQL back end. The game is based on the idea of each user having a persistent character whose skills grows along with the student. With experience, the character gains new, more powerful abilities that require the user to solve more difficult problems to unelash.
